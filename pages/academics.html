Our Academic Programs

World-Class Education in Applied Sciences and Technology

Academic Excellence at PAF-IAST

Our curriculum is designed to combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills, preparing students for success in the rapidly evolving global job market. All programs are aligned with international standards and delivered by faculty trained in leading institutions worldwide.

Our Departments

Mechanical Engineering

Curriculum focuses on machinery design, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and industrial automation with hands-on laboratory work.

  • Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering
  • Advanced Diploma in Mechanical Technology

Electrical Engineering

Specialization in power systems, control systems, renewable energy, and electrical machinery with modern lab facilities.

  • Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical Engineering
  • Advanced Diploma in Electrical Technology

Software Engineering

Training in web development, mobile apps, cloud computing, and AI/ML technologies with industry partnerships.

  • Bachelor of Engineering in Software Engineering
  • Advanced Diploma in Computer Technology

Chemical Engineering

Focus on chemical processes, environmental engineering, and sustainable technologies with practical training.

  • Bachelor of Engineering in Chemical Engineering
  • Advanced Diploma in Chemical Technology

Transportation Engineering

Specialization in railway systems, traffic engineering, and logistics management with research centers.

  • Bachelor of Engineering in Transportation Engineering
  • Master's Program in Railway Engineering

Sustainable Engineering

Education in renewable energy, environmental sustainability, and green technology solutions.

  • Bachelor of Engineering in Environmental Engineering
  • Advanced Diploma in Renewable Energy

Program Structure

Bachelor Programs

Duration: 4 Years

Total Credits: 132 Credit Hours

Components:

  • Core Engineering Courses
  • Elective Courses
  • Practical Labs
  • Final Year Project
  • Internship Semester

Diploma Programs

Duration: 2 Years

Total Credits: 64 Credit Hours

Components:

  • Specialized Technical Courses
  • Practical Training
  • Industry Projects
  • Work Experience

Master Programs

Duration: 2 Years

Total Credits: 48 Credit Hours

Components:

  • Advanced Specialization Courses
  • Research Methods
  • Thesis
  • Industry Collaboration

Admission Requirements

For Bachelor Programs

  • Intermediate (12 years) or equivalent
  • Minimum 60% aggregate marks
  • Entry test (Mathematics & Physics)
  • Valid CNIC/Passport
  • Character certificate

For Diploma Programs

  • Matriculation (10 years) or equivalent
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Basic aptitude test
  • Valid CNIC/Passport
  • Character certificate

For Master Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in related field
  • Minimum 2.5 CGPA
  • Research proposal
  • Statement of purpose
  • Recommendation letters
