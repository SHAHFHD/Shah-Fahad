Financial Aid & Scholarships

PAF-IAST offers several financial support options for deserving students:

Merit Scholarships

Awarded based on academic excellence and entrance test performance. Covers 25-100% of tuition fees.

Need-Based Financial Aid

Available for financially disadvantaged students who meet academic requirements.

International Scholarships

Special programs for international students through partner institutions in Austria and China.

Female Student Scholarships

Dedicated scholarships to encourage female participation in engineering and technology programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the minimum qualification requirements?

For bachelor programs, you need to have completed Intermediate (12 years) with minimum 60% marks. For diploma programs, Matriculation (10 years) with 50% marks is required.

When is the next admission cycle?

Applications for Spring 2026 admission open in January 2026. Fall admission typically opens in June. Check our website for exact dates.

What is the application fee?

The non-refundable application fee is PKR 2,000 for local students and USD 50 for international students.

Can I apply for multiple programs?

Yes, you can apply for up to three programs with separate application fees for each program.

Do you accept international students?

Yes, we welcome students from all over the world. International students are required to have valid passport and meet English language requirements.

What is the accommodation facility?

We provide on-campus hostel facilities for both male and female students. Separate applications are required for hostel accommodation.
