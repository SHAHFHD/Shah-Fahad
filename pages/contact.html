Frequently Asked Questions

How can I schedule a campus tour?

Contact the admissions office at admissions@paf-iast.edu.pk or call +92-995-111 723 285. Tours are available on weekdays from 10 AM to 3 PM.

What is the best way to reach the admissions office?

Email is recommended for detailed inquiries. For quick responses, call during office hours (Mon-Fri: 9 AM - 5 PM). You can also visit the office in person.

Do you have student accommodation?

Yes, we provide separate hostel facilities for male and female students. For more information, contact the Student Affairs office.

How can I request official documents/transcripts?

Visit the Registrar's office or submit a written request via email to support@paf-iast.edu.pk. Processing time is usually 3-5 working days.
