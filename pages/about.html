About PAF-IAST

Transforming Education, Building Tomorrow's Leaders

Our Story

Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology (PAF-IAST) is a purpose-built campus with state-of-the-art facilities for interactive learning through smart classrooms and advanced laboratory equipment. Located in Haripur, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, PAF-IAST stands at the forefront of technical education in Pakistan.

Our institution is committed to promoting equal opportunities and welcoming students from all backgrounds, irrespective of their creed, color, gender, ethnicity, and social status. We strongly encourage female students to join any program of their choice and realize their dreams.

Our Mission

To provide quality higher education in applied sciences and technology, with a focus on research, innovation, and practical skills development. We aim to produce graduates who are equipped to solve real-world problems and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading institution of applied sciences and technology in Asia, known for excellence in education, cutting-edge research, and strong industry partnerships. We envision a campus that nurtures innovation and entrepreneurship.
