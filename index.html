About PAF-IAST

INSTITUTE OF APPLIED SCIENCES AND TECHNOLOGY

Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology (PAF-IAST) is a public sector degree awarding institute, chartered by the Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and recognized by the Higher Education Commission of Pakistan.

PAF-IAST is committed to promoting equal opportunities and welcoming students from all backgrounds, irrespective of their creed, color, gender, ethnicity, and their social and financial status.

The institute strongly encourages female students to join any program of their choice and realize their dreams through quality education and comprehensive support services.
